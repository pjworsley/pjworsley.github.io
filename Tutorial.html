How to Use This Site

Each tutorial will take you through the step by step process of sequencing a genome; you will have to drag & drop the correct items into the correct slots in order to successfully complete a tutorial. A circular slot represents a biological process (e.g. fragmenting DNA), whereas a square slot represents a biological resource (e.g. the DNA strand itself). Every item you need can be found in the bar at the top of each page, and can be clicked on to provide further information, or - if you're stuck - a hint. Clicking the hint button will highlight the slot to which the item belongs. Below, you can practice dragging & dropping to get a feel for how the site works, you will be told when you have dropped the four item below into their correct slots. When you're done please click here return to the home page.
